What is Domestic Abuse

  • Domestic violence is a pattern of violence, abuse, or intimidation used to control or maintain power over a partner or has been in an intimate relationship.
  • Fundamentally, domestic violence is about power and control.
  • It affects people in all our communities regardless of gender, age, sexual orientation, race, ethnicity, religion, social standing and immigration status.
  • Domestic violence takes many forms: physical, emotional, economic, stalking and harassment and sexual.

Am I in an abusive relationship?

    It can be hard to decide if you are in a domestic abuse as there are many type of abuse besides pyhsical such as psychological, sexual, social and more.

    But here are some tips for you to look out for:

  • Do you feel afraid or like you're alking on eggshells when you're with your partner?
  • Does your partner control you?
  • Is there a power imbalance in your relationship?

    • If you said yes to any of the questions above. This could be a red flag.
